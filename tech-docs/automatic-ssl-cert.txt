We will get SSL automatic generation with the help of route 
annotations used here
1. Go to open shift and creat a route wth the below yaml
2. we will get generated with the SSL autoamtically



kind: Route
apiVersion: route.openshift.io/v1
metadata:
  name: nginx-proxy
  namespace: cai-chatassist-core
  annotations:
    cert-manager.io/issuer-group: adcs.certmanager.csf.nokia.com
    cert-manager.io/issuer-kind: ClusterAdcsIssuer
    cert-manager.io/issuer-name: adi-adcs-cluster-issuer
spec:
  host: nginx-proxy-cai-chatassist-core.web.analog.com
  path: /
  to:
    kind: Service
    name: nginx-proxy
    weight: 100
  port:
    targetPort: 8082
  tls:
    termination: edge
